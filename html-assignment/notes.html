5 HTML Tags

Body Tag:
This is the main body of our webpage that we see on our browser. All of the content that we want displayed on our webpage must be inside the opening and closing body tags.
Comment Tag:
This tag is a way of adding content to our webpage that will not be displayed in our browers. They are an excellent way of leaving notes for ourselves and other developers who examine our site.
Paragraph Tag:
The paragraph tag is a way is separating portions of text. Each knew paragraph will begin on a new line.
Blockquote Tag:
This tag is a way of separating a large quotation from another source.
Citation Tag:
This tag is how we cite the source of our quotations. This tag usually contains an authors names or the name of a book, article, film, etc.
